ESP32 Firmware Outline

  • Wi‑Fi provisioning (SmartConfig/captive portal)
  • MQTT/HTTP client with reconnect & backoff
  • Message queue with priority & expiry
  • Font & glyph renderer for P10
  • Scrolling engine and brightness control
  • OTA updates & watchdog

Pseudo‑Code

setup():
  load_config()
  wifi_connect()
  mqtt_connect()
  subscribe("display/{deviceId}/inbox")
  start_http_poll("/api/messages?active=true", every=60s)

loop():
  if mqtt_msg: enqueue(mqtt_msg)
  if time(): purge_expired()
  if queue not empty: render(next_msg())
