Admin Dashboard

Create, schedule, and manage notices from any browser.

Core Screens

  • Compose Notice (text, priority, expiry, speed, effects)
  • Queue & Schedule (drag to reorder, set repeat)
  • Devices (online status, brightness, last seen)
  • Audit Log & Roles (admin/editor/viewer)

Data Validations

  • Length limits, profanity filter (optional)
  • Expiry must be in the future
  • Alert messages require confirmation
