Wiring Overview (ESP32 → HUB75)

  • Data pins: R1,G1,B1,R2,G2,B2 → GPIOs
  • Address lines: A,B,C,D → GPIOs
  • CLK, LAT, OE → GPIOs
  • Power: 5V (panel), 5V→ESP32 via regulator or USB
Use a proper ground reference between ESP32 and the LED panels. Keep power cables short and thick.
