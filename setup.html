Quick Start

  1. Assemble hardware and wire ESP32 to HUB75/P10.
  2. Flash firmware with Wi‑Fi + broker/server config.
  3. Power up; device registers and shows deviceId.
  4. Open Web App → add device → send your first notice.
Tip: Start with a single panel before chaining more. Verify power headroom under full white test.

Power Budget

Estimate ~3A @ 5V per P10 panel at peak (full white). Real usage is typically lower with text.
